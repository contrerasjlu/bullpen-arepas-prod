The Arepa

Many may ask, “What is an Arepa”? It’s simple. An Arepa is a circular shaped bread made of white corn flour. Arepas can either be served baked or fried depending on the liking of the person. The Arepa is native to Venezuelan culture although you can find arepas in Colombia, Panama, Puerto Rico, Dominican Republic and the Canary Islands. At Bullpen Arepa we prepare authentic Venezuelan Arepas that are gluten free, and that filled with the best fresh ingredients and best taste.
